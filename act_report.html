Visualization and Data analysis¶

As usfeul statistics for WeRateDogs account that shows user preferences regarding retweet or favourite, shows tweets Statistics annually and followers count per year

WeRateDogs is a popular Twitter account that posts funny comments and rates of dogs from photos submitted by their followers. Active since 2015,WeRateDogs has even developed its own dog classification, based on the dogs age and appearance. Puppies are puppers, older puppers are puppos, older puppers are doggos and hairy dogs are floofers. We decided to research what the most popular dog type is, and the clear winners are pupper! We retrieved data from over 3,000 WeRateDogs tweets, including its favorite, retweet count and followers count. After some data cleaning we could see that the pupper got the highest rate.

And we can also insoect the rate of each dog type accross the years

  • It's a critical important skill to understand the data carefully to be able to pritorize what should be kept and what should be ignored

  • It's valuable to know how to represent the data in useful form for the business sides as it's a tricky way of profit

  • Data can be gathered programatically from different resources
